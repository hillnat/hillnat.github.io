About Me

I started programming at age 9, beginning with JavaScript and Scratch. A few years later I was working in Unity with C#, and continued to further my education through camps and classes while in highschool. While studying at the Academy of Interactive Entertainement Seattle, I have developed an in depth understanding of Unreal and Unity engines, associated languages, and networking for games. Since graduating I have continued my passion with various personal projects that have furthered my mastery of game development and programming.

My experience includes working with C#, C++, and C, particularly in game development and low-level programming. I’m always looking to expand my skills, take on new challenges, and refine my approach to problem-solving across different areas of development.

Beyond game programming, I’ve also developed a strong interest in electronics and robotics, working with hardware, and integrating software with physical systems.

For fun, I love flying FPV drones, playing Counter-Strike, as well as skateboarding.
