FPV Simulator [Unity, PUN2, C#]

FPV Sim is a multiplayer flight simulator designed to train FPV drone pilots in freestyle and racing scenarios. This project offers multiple freestyle maps and race courses, allowing players to refine their skills and compete for the best lap times. This project will NOT feature training for weaponized drones.

Developer

Nathan Hillger

Repo

The core objective is to provide extensive customization over drone physics and tuning, delivering a realistic and adaptable experience suited to individual user preferences. FPV pilots spend countless hours building and tuning their drones - often forgetting to train flying skills. With FPV Sim, you can set up a realistic model for your drones, and perform test flights without putting hundreds of dollars in the sky!

Additionally, FPV Sim supports a wide range of radio transmitters and gamepads, ensuring accessibility across different control methods. Designed to be lightweight, the simulator runs efficiently even on low-spec machines without dedicated GPUs.
