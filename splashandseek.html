Splash & Seek is my minor production school project for the Academy of Interactive Entertainment. The game is a bear and fish themed hide and seek, where players use various water toys and cat and mouse methods to hide and be the last survivor!

This project uses Mirror for a peer to peer networking solution, requiring no external server to route network traffic. Being one of two programmers on the project, most of my time was spent working on the networking systems as well as gameplay, where my programmer teammates were able to tackle some of the technical rendering tasks and other gameplay features.
