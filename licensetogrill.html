License to Grill [Unreal Engine 5, C++]

License to Grill is a multiplayer 3rd Person Combat game with up to 8 players. Your goal is to cook your friends in Free For All matches. Take advantage of wacky ragdoll physics to knock your friends into an an oven or tenderize them with a big hammer!

Developers

Nathan Hillger, Team Meatball (16 members)

Publisher

Academy of Interactive Entertainment

Steam Page

Use a variety of wacky and powerful weapons to cook your friends in creative ways. Each weapon has something special about it. Each weapon will heat up your opponents, eventually cooking them!

Frying Pan
Your trusty default weapon that always comes back to you even after being thrown.

Meat Hammer
A devastating Meat Tenderizer that can slam the ground with incredible power that can knock players around.

Pepper Shotgun
A powerful shotgun with strong knockback that can easily cook other players with a regular shot or a charged shot that uses more ammo.

Fridge Door
A riot shield style weapon that you can use to cool yourself faster and defend from frontal attacks as well as thrust forward with incredible speed.

Propane Launcher
A grenade launcher style weapon that fires 2 highly explosive propane tanks at once that leave fire at the point of impact.

Navigate interesting level layouts that provide plenty of opportunities for riveting interactions.

Pizza Party
A tight pizza kitchen inspired environment with a conveyor belt covered in weapons leading to a giant pizza oven that will cook you if fall in.

Market Showdown
A food market street inspired environment with a hot charcoal grill spilled in the middle of the map. Market Showdown has lots of vertical space and cover to take advantage of.

Cook the most players in a match to score the most and win!
