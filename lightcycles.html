Lightcycles is a retro-inspired game created for a school project, taking inspiration from the classic Tron franchise. It’s built with Raylib for rendering and C++ as the core language, designed to be a fun and fast-paced multiplayer experience that’s easy to play and highly portable.

Up to four players can battle it out on a single computer, or you can take on up to seven AI opponents for an extra challenge. The game gives you full control over speed and turn settings, so you can tweak things to match your playstyle. Whether you prefer tight, strategic moves or chaotic, high-speed action, Lightcycles delivers that classic light cycle battle feel.

With its retro inspired visuals and lightweight design, Lightcycles runs well on most systems. Whether playing against friends or AI, the game offers a mix of quick reactions, sharp turns, and fast-paced movement. It’s simple to get into and allows for different playstyles, making each match a unique experience.
