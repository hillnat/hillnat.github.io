Reel The Rythm [Unity, C#]

Ever play a fishing game with no fish? grab your drumsticks and your fishing rod, you'll need to keep a beat to catch these tabs!!

Developer

Nathan Hillger, AIE Game Jam Team WinterSprite

Itch

Reel the Rythm is a fishing themed rythm game, where the player must playback beats in a call in reponse fasion to help your friend get on his boat! Fish up soda tabs by succesfully matching the rythm, and help your local fishermen escape!

This game was created for a 3 day game jam at the Academy of Interactive Entertainment. The prompt was to create a one button experience. We chose to go with a fishing themed rythm game, and Reel the Rythm is the child!
